How to make vegetable biriyani

  1. Grind onion, greenchilli and ginger garlic paste
  2. Put Bayleaf, cardamom, clove, Star anise in heated oil
  3. Add the grinded paste and cook
  4. Add onion tomato and saute well
  5. Add vegetables and add rice
  6. Add 2 cups water and cook it for 3 whistles
